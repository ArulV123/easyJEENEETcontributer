Terms & Conditions

1. What this is

EasyJEENEET is a free, student-run platform where JEE and NEET aspirants share formula sheets with each other. Uploading is voluntary and content is published to help other students.

2. Ownership & rights

You must only upload material you created yourself or have the right to share. Do not upload copyrighted textbook pages, paid course material, or anyone else's copyrighted notes without permission.

3. Content standards

Uploads must be genuine academic content relevant to JEE or NEET (formulas, short notes, diagrams). We do not allow: explicit, violent, hateful, or unrelated content; personal information of others; spam or advertising; plagiarised or misleading material.

4. Moderation

All uploads may be reviewed. Our admins can remove any sheet and suspend or ban any account, without prior notice, if content violates these terms or is reported by the community. Repeated violations lead to a permanent ban.

5. Likes & reports

Other students can like your sheet or report it if it's inappropriate or incorrect. A high number of reports may result in automatic removal pending review.

6. Your account

You sign in with Google for identity verification. We store your name, uploads, and activity so students can browse contributions and admins can moderate content responsibly.

7. No warranty

Formula sheets are contributed by students and may contain errors. Always cross-check critical formulas with your textbook or teacher.

8. Changes

These terms may be updated as the platform grows. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated terms.
